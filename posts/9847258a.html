值得长时间不换的微信个性签名

发表于2023-11-09|更新于2023-11-09|分享
|字数总计:292|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:|评论数:

1.生而独一无二，无需人云亦云。

Born to be unique, no one has to follow suit

2.符合我意，一切皆好。

All is well with me

3.幸与不幸都是尽头。

Luck and misfortune are both ends

4.经事长智，历事成人。

After a long time wisdom, all things become adults

5.明智的放弃胜过盲目的执着。

It is better to give up wisely than to persist blindly

6.随心所欲才是公主的作风。

It is the Princess’s way to do as she pleases

7.慢品人间烟火色，闲观万事岁月长。

Slow Taste of human fireworks, idle view of all things long time

8.不让别人为难，也不让自己委屈。

Do not let others difficult, also do not let oneself aggrieved

9.虽偶有轻风细雨，但总归晴天朗照。

Although there is a light wind and rain now and then, it is always sunny

10.别听世俗的耳语，去看喜欢的风景。

Don’t listen to the Whisper of the world, to see the scenery you like

11.自由和快乐才是人生的主旨。

Freedom and happiness are the essence of life

12.门庭深冷，来者需诚。

The door is cold, the visitor needs sincerity

13.外向是生活所需，孤独是自我享受。

Extroversion is the need of life, loneliness is self-enjoyment
